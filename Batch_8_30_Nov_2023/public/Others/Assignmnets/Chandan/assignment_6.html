Name: Rohit sharma

Age: 36

Role: Batsman

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer and the current captain of India national cricket team in all formats.Considered one of the best batsmen of his generation and one of greatest opening batters of all time,[3] Sharma is known for his timing, elegance, six-hitting abilities and leadership skills. He plays as a right-handed batsman for India national cricket team in international cricket, Mumbai Indians in IPL and for Mumbai in domestic cricket. .

Name: shubman gill

Age: 24

Role: Batsman

Shubman Gill is an Indian cricketer who plays for the Indian national team in all three formats of the game. He is considered one of the most promising young batsmen in the world. Gill has a double century to his name in ODIs and the highest T20I score for India. He also holds the record for the fastest player to score 2000 runs in ODIs. Gill made his international debut in January 2019. He was part of the India Under-19 team that won the 2018 Cricket World Cup and was named the Player of the Tournament. In 2022, Gill was part of the IPL championship winning Gujarat Titans team. .
